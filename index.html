Our Mission and Vision

At the Psychological Healing Center, we are dedicated to guiding individuals on a transformative journey from past healing to future empowerment. Our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing environment where clients can explore their emotional landscapes, heal from trauma, and break free from toxic patterns. We envision a world where individuals are empowered to decode their past and recode their future, fostering personal healing and emotional growth & mastery through our unique Mind Map Therapy.

The Mind Map Therapy Journey

01

Encoding: How Your Wounds of Childhood Were Encoded

Wound

The main wounds of childhood include verbal abuse, physical abuse and neglect, sexual abuse, control and smothering, and narcissistic parenting.

Reactions

When we are wounded, our lens of perception cracks, our reality distorts, and in reaction we either fight, flee, freeze or fawn.

Encoding

As a result of these childhood wounds we encode negative core beliefs that act as emotional triggers that destroy our self-esteem.

02

Decoding: Understanding Your Patterns

Chaos

When negative core beliefs are triggered, they break us down into chaos.

Defenses

We erect defenses to protect ourselves against pain, however some defenses lock us into psychological prison.

Breakdown / Breakthrough

When we can no longer defend ourselves through dysfunctional means, we break down, which is an opportunity to breakthrough.

03

Recoding: Transform Your Future

Paradigm Shift

Results when you breakthrough, heal your cracked lens of perception, and arrive at a new way of seeing and a new way of being. As you recode into health, you evolve into a better version of yourself.

Unity

When you break the multi-generational encoding of psychopathology, you can be the cause of better outcomes for your life and pay it forward to the next generation.
